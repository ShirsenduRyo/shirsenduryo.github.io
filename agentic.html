Retrieval

Knowledge-grounded assistant

A useful assistant should respond from trusted context, not just from model memory. This pattern is ideal for documentation-heavy internal knowledge or domain-specific support.

System idea

Retrieve, rank, cite, and answer with strong source grounding.

Why it matters

Improves trust and reduces the risk of ungrounded responses.

Analytics

Analyst copilot

This pattern accelerates repetitive analysis work while keeping a human analyst in control of framing, validation, and communication.

System idea

Connect natural-language prompting to common analysis tools and templates.

Why it matters

Creates faster iteration loops without hiding the reasoning behind outputs.

Automation

Workflow orchestration layer

Many business processes are a sequence of small handoffs. Agentic workflows shine when they reduce the glue work between those steps.

System idea

Combine tool calls, approval states, and retry logic into one flow.

Why it matters

Removes manual coordination overhead and improves process speed.

Support

Support triage assistant

A triage layer can reduce repetitive handling while ensuring that ambiguous or sensitive issues still reach the right human owner.

System idea

Classify, enrich, route, and draft response suggestions for review.

Why it matters

Gives support teams more time for higher-complexity conversations.

Evaluation

Prompt and answer evaluation loop

Good AI systems need evaluation habits, not just demos. This pattern makes performance visible and easier to improve over time.

System idea

Track prompts, compare outputs, and score answers against defined criteria.

Why it matters

Helps teams manage drift, quality regression, and stakeholder trust.

Governance

Human-aware agent guardrails

Strong AI UX includes boundaries, review states, and fallback paths. Guardrails are part of the product, not an afterthought.

System idea

Blend confidence checks, approvals, logging, and controlled tool access.

Why it matters

Supports safer adoption in workflows that affect customers or operations.
