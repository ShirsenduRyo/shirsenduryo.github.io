Framing

Business problem translation

Strong data science often starts before the model. Framing matters because the wrong objective can still produce technically correct but practically useless work.

What it looks like

Clarifying goals, choosing metrics, and defining the actual decision to support.

Why it matters

Good scoping reduces wasted effort and keeps the work tied to outcomes.

Modeling

Predictive modeling

Modeling is most useful when it balances performance, interpretability, and how the result will actually be consumed downstream.

What it looks like

Feature selection, baseline comparison, evaluation design, and practical deployment thinking.

Why it matters

Turns historical data into a tool for ranking, forecasting, or targeting.

Measurement

Experimentation and analysis

Statistical thinking helps teams separate real effects from noise and make stronger decisions under uncertainty.

What it looks like

Designing comparisons, checking assumptions, and reading results carefully.

Why it matters

Keeps teams from overreacting to noisy swings or underestimating real change.

Analytics

Exploration and reporting

Not every business problem needs a complex model. Often the win comes from finding the right insight and making it visible in time.

What it looks like

EDA, visual summaries, trend interpretation, and dashboard-friendly communication.

Why it matters

Supports faster, better decisions without unnecessary complexity.

Communication

Stakeholder-facing delivery

Strong analysis only matters if people can understand it, trust it, and act on it.

What it looks like

Clear summaries, grounded recommendations, and role-appropriate explanations.

Why it matters

Improves adoption and keeps technical work connected to real decisions.

Workflow

From analysis to reusable systems

Reusable systems create leverage. Even traditional data science work is stronger when it can be repeated, monitored, or operationalized.

What it looks like

Repeatable workflows, cleaner handoffs, and outputs designed for ongoing use.

Why it matters

Moves work from one-off analysis toward durable business capability.
